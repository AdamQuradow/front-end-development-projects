About Us

Who we are

AgriStore is an agriculture startup located in Tamale in the northen region of Ghana. AgriStore was established in August, 2022 as agriculture company that leverage digital technology to connect Farmers to consumers (customers) in the northern region. Our mision is to bring food products to the doorsteps of consumers and to provide ready market for farmers to reduce post-harvest losess.

Who we do

AgriStore help farmers to sell their farm products on its website. Farm Products on the website are food that cultivated in the northern sector of Ghana. We deal in variety of products such as Tubers (yam, cassava, ginger and potatoea), Legumes (soyabeans, beans, cowpea and bambara beans), cereal and grains (rice, maize, sorghum and millet), Vegetables (cabbage, pepper carrot, cucumber, lettuce and onion) and Fruits fruits (mango and Tomatoes)
