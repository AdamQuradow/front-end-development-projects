The Advantages of an OpenLabs Education

Formerly known as NIIT Ghana, OpenLabs are technology centers across West Africa aimed at improving career outcomes and digitally upskilling th youth of Africa, having trained over 30,000 students in Ghana, Liberia & Sierra Leone

Our most popular programs are the 24 months Diploma courses

Immersive technology curriculums designed to bring out the engineer in you and prepare you for the most demanding jobs in tech. They are recognized by Corporates across banking, technology, startups amd enterprise technology

OpenLabs diplomas give you the flexibility to top-up your studies at universities in the UK & Ghana, to obtain a university degree

OpenLabs's classroom based curriculums have been taught since 1991 across 5,000+ centers globally and seasnally updated to maintain relevance in the changing landscape of technology

All learners have access to:-

  • High speed complimentary internet access
  • Windows workstation with all the relevant softwares, tools and packages
  • Library and student Lounge
  • Data Centre and Virtual Machines
  • Gaming worktation (e.g Fornite)
  • Learning resource from Cisco, Microsoft, Fortinet & IBM
